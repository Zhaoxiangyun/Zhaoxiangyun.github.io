About Me

Hello! My name is Xiangyun Zhao. I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate studying Computer Vision and Machine Learning at the EECS department @ Northwestern University. My advisor is Professor Ying Wu.

I am interested in exploring and solving important problems in computer vision. I have tried to solve some interesting problems in some computer vision applications, such as object detection, segmentation, image retrieval and face recognition. My research is problem-oriented, so it is not limited to specific applications. I am more interested in solving some general problems which may exist in different applications. Currently, in order to make large-scale recognition achievable, I am working on improving the learning efficiency with insufficient training data.

I as very fortunate to have interned at 360 AI Institute(Beijing, 2015), Microsoft Research Asia (Beijing, 2016), Adobe Research (San Jose, 2017) and Baidu Research (Sunnyvale, 2018).
